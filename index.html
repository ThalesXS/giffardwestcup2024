Bússola Quebrada

By: Rafael Galhardo

"Your shoulders bear too much weight
Tonight the sky collapsed and you didn't even notice
Your shoulders bear too much weight
Tonight the sky collapsed and you didn't even notice"

"Teus ombros suportam pesos demais
Essa noite o céu desabou e você nem percebeu
Teus ombros suportam pesos demais
Essa noite o céu desabou e você nem percebeu"

Ingredient: Ginger

  The ginger reference symbolizes immune reinforcement and the willingness to deal with difficult nights, important for bar professionals who deal with an intense volume of work. The lyrics suggest that, despite being overloaded, they continue to perform their duties with dedication and energy, without realizing the weight of the burden.

"Steel Handcuffs, Copperfield's Illusion
Sobriety is a Holyfield Punch
My shelter is your arms
Ties that secure the vessel to the bridge"

"Algemas de aço, Ilusão Copperfield
A sobriedade é um Soco do Holyfield
Meu abrigo é teus braços
Laços que prendem a embarcação na ponte"

Ingredient: Cachaça

  This reference demystifies the association of cachaça with alcoholism, highlighting it as a symbol of comfort and national identity. The comparison of steel handcuffs with Copperfield's illusion and Holyfield's sobriety as a punch highlight the duality of drinking between freedom and prison. The "ties that bind the vessel to the bridge" suggest an emotional connection with Brazil, reinforcing its origins.

"Your black eyes remind me of my early mornings
This life is a Penrose Triangle
Where I photographed it in my Pin-hole, solitude"

"Teus olhos negros lembram minhas madrugadas em claro
Essa vida é um Triângulo de Penrose
Onde eu o fotografei na minha Pin-hole, solidão"

Ingredient: Salt and Smoked Bitter

  The analogy between the complex homemade bitter and life as a Penrose Triangle suggests an intrinsic complexity to existence, comparable to the paradoxical nature of the Penrose Triangle. Bitter, with its salty and smoky flavor, represents the nuances and contrasts of life, including moments of bitterness and depth. The reference to black eyes reminiscent of late nights indicates an association between hard work for many people and at the same time loneliness, suggesting a search for meaning or understanding amid the complexity of existence.

"On the edge of the bar, another sun that dies
My heart a broken compass
I lie down on this starry floor that invades the living room of the house
Subtly I touch this sky of souls"

"Na orla da barra mais um sol que morre
Meu coração uma bússola quebrada
Me deito nesse chão estrelado que invade a sala da casa
Sutilmente eu toco nesse céu de almas"

Ingredient: Elderflower Liqueur

  The elderflower liqueur, with its delicacy and floral aroma, represents a kind of tribute to the souls who passed through the bar, as customers or friends. The starry floor reflects the interactions and energy exchanges that occur in the bar environment, creating a unique and special atmosphere. Highlighting the depth of shared experiences and connections formed around a bar counter, transforming it into a place of meeting and memories.
